About Us

Who We Are

IGreenArmy is a volunteer-driven environmental group dedicated to cleaner streets, greener neighborhoods, and informed citizens.

Our Mission

We organize public place cleaning drives, tree plantation campaigns, and awareness events that empower local communities.

Why It Matters

Cleaner surroundings reduce health risks and greener cities fight climate change while making life better for everyone.

Volunteer Impact

Every volunteer contributes meaningful hours that turn into cleaner spaces, more trees, and stronger community participation.
